About Us

Welcome to The Factory on Barclay, a distinguished venue nestled in the heart of Milwaukee's historic Walker's Point district. Housed in a beautifully restored 1909 Cream City brick building, our venue seamlessly blends industrial charm with modern elegance.

Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or special celebration, our versatile spaces offer the perfect backdrop for your occasion.

Our Spaces

  • The Main Factory: A grand space featuring high bay ceilings and exposed beams, perfect for large-scale events.
  • The Warehouse: Ideal for intimate gatherings, with cathedral ceilings and natural light.
  • The Mezzanine: Overlooking the main floor, perfect for cocktail hours or additional seating.
